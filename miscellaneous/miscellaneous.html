Miscellaneous

This are the jobs which are unknown to students

Cybersecurity Auditor

Cybersecurity auditors work with companies and organizations to provide comprehensive audits of online security systems

Professional Chef

A professional chef directs food preparation and oversees kitchen operations in restaurants and other dining establishments.

Event Planner

An event planneis a responsible for organising and coordinating meetings and special events for individuals, businesses, non-profit organisations or other groups.

Food Critic

A food critic is a writer who specializes in providing thoughtful commentary on their experiences dining out.

Interior Designer

An interior designer is responsible for the layout of the internal space in a building or structure.

Fitness Trainer

Certified personal trainers help others achieve their health and fitness goals through safe, efficient workout programs.

Disc Jockey

Disc jockeys tend to work for radio stations performing on-air duties, such as playing music, reading commercials, interviewing guests and, sometimes, taking song requests over the phone from listeners.

Ethical Hacker

Ethical hacker keeps the data secures and prevents it from being stolen and misused by malicious hackers. Ethical hacking is a legitimate and legal profession.

Theripist

A therapist is a licensed mental health professional (LMHP) who treats clients with a range of mental health conditions, enabling them to develop coping mechanisms, improve emotional skills and ease symptoms

Patient Examiner

Patent examiners use their scientific, technical and legal skills to assess applications for patents, which are granted to applicants to give them the right to stop other people using, selling or making their inventions

Private investigator

Private investigators are usually employed by law enforcement agencies or private clients to help with legal, financial and personal matters. They often track down information or people who prove difficult to find.

Air Traffic Controller

An Air Traffic Controller is in charge of managing the flow of air machines in the sky, whether they are fighters, commercial planes, airbus planes or other planes.
