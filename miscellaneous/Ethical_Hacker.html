Ethical Hacker

Overview:

Ethical hacker keeps the data secures and prevents it from being stolen and misused by malicious hackers. Ethical hacking is a legitimate and legal profession. Many times website that has constant public traffic and also stores customer data as likely to get hacked. In such cases, the organizations hire an ethical hacker who can find out and block such loopholes that would give access to an unethical hacker.

Education:

Student can do 12-Science. Then complete B.Sc in Computer Science. Further you can Proceed with Certification course in Ethical Hacking (CEH. Further you can Proceed with CCNA. Further you can Proceed with SCNS. Further you can Proceed with CPTE. Further you can Proceed with CISSP).There are also some other path can be chacked in resources.
