Theripist

Overview:

A therapist is a licensed mental health professional (LMHP) who treats clients with a range of mental health conditions, enabling them to develop coping mechanisms, improve emotional skills and ease symptoms

Education:

Most therapy jobs require a master's degree in counseling, psychology or the practice area they want to pursue, such as marriage and family counseling. Candidates often earn a bachelor's degree in psychology, social work or a related field. This allows them to gain the foundational knowledge that can help them succeed in the field.
